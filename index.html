Experience

Head of Product & Design @ Archer

May - September 2018

Archer worked to develop a platform that combined the power of human insight with the scalability of computation in order to change how everyday people view, analyze, understand, and publish information. My role was highly crossfunctional, as I worked with everything from UI/UX to backend to data engineering. My favorite technological challenge was architecting a knowledge graph that could store & reconcile people's different models of the world, allowing humans and computers to extract useful and actionable information.

August 2017 - May 2018

Archer Labs is a nonprofit focused on applying tech to issues of public benefit. During my time, I lead the team that developed SanctionsExplorer, the most modern publicly-available platform (and most loved by the community!) for searching and understanding U.S. Treasury sanctions. Check out my design portfolio for more information on this one!

Software Engineering Intern (Product) @ Tanium

June - August 2017

As an intern, I developed a plug-and-play NodeJS module that could be integrated into a number of separate (but similar) products that Tanium offered. This module allowed developers to specify which metrics should be exposed to customers; these metrics would then be collected and displayed (which was more difficult than it sounds, given the arbitrarity of the data) for technical and nontechnical customers.
